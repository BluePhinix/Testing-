Neo is a Minecraft Bedrock Edition addon developer and content creator from Bangladesh, running the YouTube channel **NeoPhinixMC**, where he publishes addon showcases and tutorials in Bangla. He's built an impressive catalog of addons — from chaos wands to quality-of-life tweaks and custom enchanting tables — releasing them on platforms like CurseForge and MCPEDL. Beyond Minecraft, he's a Linux-only guy with a growing interest in JavaScript.
